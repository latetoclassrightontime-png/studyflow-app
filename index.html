Privacy & Data
🔒
Your data stays on your device
StudyFlow stores all your information — courses, assignments, schedules, grades, and notes — locally on this device only. Nothing is uploaded to external servers. No account is required. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties.
📋 What StudyFlow stores
• Your name — used to personalize the app
• School & semester info — to organize your schedule
• Courses, assignments & grades — stored locally only
• Work schedule & study preferences — used to auto-build your calendar
• Sticky notes & timer logs — stored locally only
🚫 We do not collect location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any device identifiers.
🤖
AI Feature Disclosure
The AI Syllabus Import feature (Me → Settings → AI Syllabus Import) sends the text you paste or upload to Anthropic's Claude AI to extract course info and due dates. This text is sent securely over HTTPS. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to data processed by their API.
By using AI Syllabus Import, you consent to your syllabus text being processed by Anthropic's Claude API. No other data is ever sent to AI services.

🤖 AI Feature Consent
The AI Syllabus Import feature uses Anthropic's Claude AI to read and extract information from your syllabus. Here is exactly what that means:
What gets sent: Only the syllabus text you paste or upload.
Who receives it: Anthropic, PBC (makers of Claude AI).
Why: To extract course name, due dates, and schedule so you don't have to type them manually.
What is NOT sent: Your name, school, grades, schedule, notes, or any other app data.
Anthropic's policy: anthropic.com/privacy
You can use all other features of StudyFlow without ever using AI Syllabus Import. Your consent here only applies to that specific feature.
Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2025
1. Overview
StudyFlow is designed with privacy first. We do not run servers, require accounts, or collect, transmit, or sell personal data.
2. Data We Store (On-Device Only)
All data — your name, school, courses, assignments, grades, schedule, work shifts, study preferences, notes, and timer history — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly use calendar export or AI Syllabus Import.
3. Data We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect: your real name or email, location or GPS data, contacts or calendar access, camera or microphone data, device identifiers, advertising IDs, cookies, or analytics data.
4. AI Feature (Syllabus Import)
When you choose to use AI Syllabus Import, the text you provide is sent to Anthropic's Claude API over a secure HTTPS connection. Only the text you submit is transmitted — no other app data is included. We obtain your explicit in-app consent before any data is sent. You may revoke consent at any time in Settings.
5. Calendar Export
Calendar export generates a .ics file on your device. No data is sent to our servers.
6. Children's Privacy
Kids Mode is designed for learners ages 5 and up with age-appropriate content. We do not collect any personal information from children. All data remains on-device. Parents may delete all data anytime in Settings.
7. Your Rights and Data Deletion
You may delete all data at any time via Settings. Since we have no servers, this is the only deletion needed and is immediate and permanent.
8. Third-Party Services
The only third-party service used is Anthropic's Claude API (only with your explicit consent). We use no advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social SDKs.

Questions: support@latetoclass.app
